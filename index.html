Gift Wrapping Paper Calculator

Use this calculator to determine the dimensions to cut your wrapping paper to just the right size

Directions:

  1. Enter the dimensions of the box you are wrapping (length x width x height)
  2. You can adjust how much overlap of the wrapping paper you want at the seams
  3. Measurements can be in inches or centimeters
  4. Click calculate and you'll get back the dimensions you should cut your paper to
